About Microsoft Student Ambassadors

Building tomorrow's tech leaders today

The Microsoft Student Ambassador program empowers students around the world to learn, grow, and make a difference. Through hands-on experience with Microsoft technologies and exclusive resources, ambassadors develop technical and career skills while building a strong network within the global tech community.

Technical Excellence

Access to Microsoft Azure credits, developer tools, and training resources to build cutting-edge solutions.

Leadership Development

Opportunities to lead workshops, mentor peers, and organize tech events in your community.

Global Network

Connect with fellow ambassadors, Microsoft professionals, and industry experts worldwide.

Career Growth

Exclusive internship opportunities, resume building, and professional development resources.
